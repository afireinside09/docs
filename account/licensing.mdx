## License Types

SheetMagic offers three license durations:

| Type | Duration | Renewal |
|------|----------|---------|
| Monthly | 1 month | Auto-renews |
| Annual | 1 year | Auto-renews |
| Lifetime | Forever | One-time purchase |

## Activating Your License

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open SheetMagic">
    In Google Sheets, go to **Extensions > SheetMagic > Start SheetMagic**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter License Key">
    Paste your license key in the activation dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Enter the email associated with your license.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Click Activate to verify your license.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## License Verification

SheetMagic verifies your license:
- When you first activate
- Periodically during use (cached for 30 days)
- When using AI functions

<Note>
License verification is cached to ensure fast performance. You can use SheetMagic offline after initial verification.
</Note>

## Managing Your License

### View License Status

In Google Sheets: **Extensions > SheetMagic > License Status**

This shows:
- License key
- License type
- Expiration date (if applicable)
- Seat information

### Update License

To change your license key:
1. Go to **Extensions > SheetMagic > License Status**
2. Enter your new license key
3. Click Update

## License Status

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Active | License is valid and in good standing |
| Cancelled | Subscription cancelled, valid until period ends |
| Suspended | License suspended, contact support |
| Expired | License has expired, renewal required |

## Subscription Management

For monthly and annual licenses:

### Manage Billing

1. Log in to [sheetmagic.ai/dashboard](https://sheetmagic.ai/dashboard)
2. Click **Manage Subscription**
3. Access the Stripe billing portal

From the portal you can:
- Update payment method
- View invoices
- Change subscription tier
- Cancel subscription

### Cancel Subscription

1. Go to the billing portal
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Choose cancellation timing

<Note>
Cancelled subscriptions remain active until the end of the current billing period.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="License key not accepted">
    - Verify the key is copied correctly (case-sensitive)
    - Check for extra spaces before or after the key
    - Ensure you're using the email associated with the purchase
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="License shows as expired">
    - Check your subscription status in the dashboard
    - Verify payment method is up to date
    - Contact support if payment was successful but license expired
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't access features">
    - Verify license is active in License Status
    - Try reactivating the license
    - Clear cache with **Reset All Data** and reactivate
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Multiple devices">
    SheetMagic licenses work across all your devices. Just enter the same license key in each Google Sheets document.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## License Security

- Keys are stored encrypted in your Google account
- Each key is tied to your email address
- Sharing keys violates terms of service
